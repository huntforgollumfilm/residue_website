Design Competition

If you think you have a good eye for design and would like to get involved in the The Hunt For Gollum project, then our design competition could be for you!

We're looking for people to design posters, flyers, wallpapers and logos around the The Hunt For Gollum theme. The best entries (selected by the producers' at their discretion) will be showcased here on the website, get a credit on the film and potentially be used as promotional material!

 How To Enter

You can use any images on the Human Residue website in your designs (there is an additional .zip file below filled with other images you can use), just open up them up in your favourite graphic design program and get to work. You're welcome to use your own images, or images from other sources so long as you aren't using any copywrited material.

 

The Hunt For Gollum - Design Competition Images (12MB .zip file)

 

Once you are done, simply send your finished work as a high quality .jpeg file to mike@hunt4gollum.com by the 1st of August 2006 along with your name.
 
Winners work will be continually announced up until shortly after the submission deadline.
