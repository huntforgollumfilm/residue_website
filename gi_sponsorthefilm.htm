Donate to the production budget


 

Click the button above to donate to the production.  

We want as many people to be able to enjoy The Hunt For Gollum for free, but even on our shoestring budget and with a cast and crew made up entirely of volunteers, a film like this is expensive to produce, especially for a bunch of nobodies without any big budget funding. The biggest costs fall under the art department budget, props and costumes. We want these to look really authentic.

Click the 'PayPal' button above to donate to The Hunt For Gollum and we will send you a special DVD of the finished film upon completion. As the film will not be available to buy, this is the only way to get a DVD (of course it will be free to download as well!). Please also email info @ hunt4gollum.com to let us know your contact details if you want a DVD*.

NOTE: The Hunt For Gollum is an entirely non-profit film and the filmmakers are not making a penny from the production. It is a labour of love by fans for fans, so any donations are warmly appreciated. Once our target budget has been reached (£2500) we will close the website to donations.

We guarantee that 100% of the money donated will be put into the production and will have a direct impact on the quality of the film.
