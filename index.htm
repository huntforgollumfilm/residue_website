Welcome to the official website for the 'Human Residue' project!

About the project

Human Residue is a survival concept movie from filmmaker Chris Bouchard, developed with collaborator Mike Ritchie and Rickety Shack Films in 2006. The trailers and clips on this site offer a sneak peek into this post apocalyptic world. Since then Chris has gone on to make the Lord of the Rings fan film sensation: The Hunt For Gollum.
